# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Agentklar to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone, regardless of
experience, background, or identity. Contributors and maintainers pledge to make
participation a respectful experience for all.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps:

- Being kind, patient, and welcoming to newcomers.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insults, discriminatory or derogatory
comments, personal or political attacks, publishing others' private information, and any
conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any
official channel — and when an individual is representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to **conduct@kaltstart.co**. Reports will be reviewed and
handled confidentially. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments,
and contributors that violate this code.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
